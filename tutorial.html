Tutorial: The Art of the Prompt

The quality of an AI's output is directly proportional to the quality of its input. Learning to "prompt" effectively is the single most important skill for working with modern LLMs. Let's learn by doing.

A good prompt has three key ingredients:

  1. Role: Tell the AI who it should be. (e.g., "You are a helpful botanist.")
  2. Task: Tell the AI exactly what you want it to do. (e.g., "Describe the five best houseplants for low-light apartments.")
  3. Format: Tell the AI how you want the output structured. (e.g., "Provide the answer as a numbered list with a brief explanation for each.")
